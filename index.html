What technologies are you using?

Real Items utilizes blockchains like Ethereum, Polygon, and Vechain, peer-to-peer technology like IPFS, and cryptography like Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs) in a decentralized information storage and communication platform.

How is Real Items different from its competitors?

Our competitors utilize permissioned blockchains which lack trust, use a single blockchain network which lacks flexibility, or operate on environmentally unsustainable blockchains like Ethereum which lack the scalability to process high volumes of inventory at low cost. Real Items is a multichain protocol.

Blockchains consume energy and enable climate change. How are you sustainable?

Real Items is integrated with carbon neutral chains like Polygon (going carbon negative in 2022) and Vechain with more on the way. Every company has different needs and Real Items is set up sustainably for you to succeed.
